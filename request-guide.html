Request Guide

Miigwetch for your interest in buying custom beadwork from me!

Please take a read-through before sending in a request email so I can get as close as possible to what you want.

*There are no returns/refunds on custom-made products and payment must be made before I start the project*

Pattern

For a custom pattern, please attach pixel art (physical or digital) to your email. For the colours, it would be best to go through your colours and add the co-ordinates of where they are in my beadboxes (ie. 4c).

Size

In your email, please specify how long you'd like the actual beadwork aspect and if you don't have a particular design, please also write how many beads thick you'd like it. For bookmarks, write as well the height of your book :)

Type

At the moment, I make:

  • Bookmarks
  • Bracelets
  • Brick-Stitch Fringe Earrings
  • Brick-Stitch Loop Fringe Earrings
  • Brick-Stitch Rings/Tubes

If there's something you want that I don't have listed above, shoot me an email :) I might be able to do it for you

If you're not picky and just want to choose one or more different aspects, please let me know so I know you didn't forget something :)

Pricing

All prices are in CAD

  • Per 1 Hour of Work - 5$
  • More than 5 colours - 1.50$ per extra colour
  • More than 15 beads thick - 1$ per extra bead
  • Earrings 2"x1" or smaller - 15$
  • Earrings 2.1" - 3" height - 25$
  • Earrings 3.1" - 4" height - 35$
  • Earrings taller than 4" - TBD upon email

Bookmarks have a 4$ extra fee due to the waistband elastic used.

I will quote you for the price in the form of an item list :)
