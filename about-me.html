Why the name "Raven and Gus"?

Despite this being a one-person operation, I called my business "Raven and Gus" after my cat, Gus, who means the world to me. I found him on the side of the road when he was about 4 weeks old, starving, absolutely infested with ear-mites and lice, and pretty sick. I myself wasn't doing so hot, and I consider him a gift from the spirits because I needed him at that time just as much as he needed me. My small beautiful boy is now 4 years old and has more energy than our younger cats :)

He's a very smart, kind, gentle, playful, thoughtful prankster and I genuinely don't know what I'd do without him. His picture is in the dictionary under the term "Good Boy", no need to email Merriam-Webster as this is the correct definition.

We are currently stationed on Cape Breton Island until the spring, so this is where it will be shipping from! I will post an update on Instagram when/if our housing situation changes and if it does, we'll be back in Ontario (central or southern). Apologies for any shipping inconvenience in advance!
